2021-2022

On campus, I am involved in a 501(c) non-profit organization called Liberty in North Korea in which I am the External Vice President for our UMich rescue team chapter. Our main objectives are to bring awareness to the current situation on North Korean refugees, raise funds for their journey of escaping to rehabilitation, and create a sense of belonging for our tight-knit community of students.



I am also involved in Multi-Cultural Greek life through my Asian-interest sorority, alpha Kappa Delta Phi. I serve as the Vice President of Finance where I dedicate my time and efforts in handling all aspects of our chapter's funds and financial obligations. Our sorority's purpose is to raise Asian-Awareness, leadership, scholarship, sisterhood, and service.
