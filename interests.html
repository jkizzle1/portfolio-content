Swimming

I have been a competitive swimmer for thirteen years. Some notable mentions are that I was the GA State Record Holder (Girls 9-10yo, 50 yard Backstroke), GA 7A High School State Winner (200 Medley Relay), two time GA Zone Team member, and multiple first places at the GA State Championships (long course and short course).
